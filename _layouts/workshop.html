Workshop on Decoding Decisions: Explainability in ML & Sequential Decision Making

Workshop at The Conferece on Robots and Vision (CRV) 2025

Machine learning models are increasingly deployed in high-stakes decision-making processes across various domains, including healthcare, finance, and autonomous systems. However, the opaque nature of these models raises concerns about accountability, trustworthiness, and ethical implications. This workshop aims to advance research at the intersection of explainability and sequential decision-making by exploring novel methodologies for interpreting AI-driven decisions, understanding their causal mechanisms, and ensuring fairness and robustness in dynamic environments.
